Business freedom

Business freedom

An overall indicator of the efficiency of government regulation of business. The quantitative score is obtained from an array of measurements of the difficulty of starting, operating, and closing a business in that area.

Corruption Freedom

an index which ranks countries "by their perceived levels of public sector corruption, as determined by expert assessments and opinion surveys.

Labor Restrictions

forbids employers from interfering with, restraining, or coercing employees in the exercise of rights relating to organizing, forming, joining or assisting a labor organization

The the concentration of individuals within a species in a specific geographic city’s center.

UA Population Density

the concentration of individuals within a species in an urban area

UA Population Size

The number of individuals in an urban area population

Rent Index Telescore

Consumer Price Index

a measure of the average change over time in the prices paid by urban consumers for a market basket of consumer goods and services.

Economy

GDP Growth Rate

annual average rate of change of the GDP at market prices based on constant local currency, for a given national economy, during a specified period of time.

GDP Per Capita

sum of gross value added by all resident producers in the economy plus any product taxes not included in the valuation of output, divided by mid-year population.

Education

PISA Ranking

measures 15-year-olds' ability to use their reading, mathematics and science knowledge and skills to meet real-life challenges.

PISA Ranking Telescore

The mean score of the PISA (student assessment)

Student Happiness

A measurement calculated by taking the percentage of unhappy students from the percentage of happy students based on the ratings surveyed.

Cost

Quality

Air Pollution

Used by government agencies to communicate to the public how polluted the air currently is or how polluted it is forecast to become.

Cleanliness

Index telling you how clean or polluted your air is and what associated health effects might be a concern for you

Water Quality

a tool developed by scientists to help evaluate the quality of water in these streams and rivers.

Crime Rate

Calculates safety of a city based on homicide, theft, assault, robbery and motor vehicle theft
